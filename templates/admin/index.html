WE WANT TO GIVE PIANISTS MORE OPPORTUNITIES TO PLAY FOR THE PUBLIC.



This outreach project is about developing more ways for people to see, hear and enjoy the piano in action.


It began as a pop-up concert series with one piano on one street corner -- created to break down the barrier between performers and audience members, between professional musicians and students, amateurs and those who just enjoy listening.




Day or night, people gather around the piano and play.
